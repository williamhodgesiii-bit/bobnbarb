The Evening

The other half
of the house

When the daytime counter goes quiet, the room is transformed. Candlelight and low vinyl, pressed linen, and a single seating for those in the know — the considered, unhurried other half of Bob & Barb's, entirely by reservation.

There is no menu to order from. The chef composes a seven-course tasting each evening from Gulf oysters, Delta catfish, dry-aged beef and whatever the season offers — Mississippi cooking, quietly elevated, and met glass for glass from a reserve cellar.

No rush. No spectacle. Just an evening set aside for the table in front of you.
