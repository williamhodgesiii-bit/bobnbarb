The Story

New sign. Same attitude.

Bob & Barb's earned its name one plate at a time — the kind of place where folks call ahead, line up, and leave with sauce on their Sunday best. Now we're firing up Q'n 2 in downtown Tupelo, same recipes, same big flavor, brand-new pit.

It's done the only way that's ever made sense to us: hardwood smoke, salt and pepper, and time. Brisket and pulled pork low and slow, smash burgers with a lacy crispy edge, sides made from scratch. No shortcuts. Don't be afraid of flavor.

And on a few special weekend nights, we kill the lights, light the candles, and the pit becomes a supper club — white tablecloths, a chef's menu, and barbecue that wears a tie.
