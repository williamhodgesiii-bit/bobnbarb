The Story

Same name. Same fire. New zip code.

Bob & Barb's started as a little smokehouse with a big reputation — the kind of place where folks lined up before the doors opened and left with sauce on their Sunday best. We packed up the pit, kept the recipes, and rolled into downtown Tupelo to do it all over again.

Everything's still done the only way that's ever made sense to us: post-oak and hickory, salt and pepper, and time — 16 hours of it, give or take. No shortcuts. No microwaves. Just smoke that knows your name.

And on a few special weekend nights, we kill the lights, light the candles, and the pit becomes a supper club — white tablecloths, a chef's menu, and barbecue that wears a tie.
