Who we are

New sign,
same cookin'

Bob & Barb's earned its name one plate at a time — the kind of place folks call ahead for, line up for, and leave with sauce on their good shirt. Now we've fired up Q'n 2 right in downtown Tupelo.

We do it the only way that's ever made sense to us: brisket and pulled pork over hardwood, low and slow. Smash burgers with a lacy, crispy edge. Sides made from scratch every morning. No shortcuts, no fuss.
